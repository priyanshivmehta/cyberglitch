    Discover the cosmic enigmas

    Uncover how these celestial objects challenge our understanding of space and time, and learn about the latest discoveries and theories that continue to captivate scientists and enthusiasts alike.

    Wormhole

    A theoretical passage through space-time that creates shortcuts for long
    journeys across the universe.

    Black Hole

    A region of space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even
    light, can escape from it.

    Quasars

    Extremely luminous active galactic nuclei, powered by supermassive black holes.
    These distant and powerful objects shine brighter than entire galaxies.

    Timeline of Space Exploration

    Pre-Space Age (Before 1957)


    • Date: Various dates before 1957


    Events:

    • Early rocketry experiments by Konstantin Tsiolkovsky and Robert Goddard.

    • Formation of space agencies like NASA and USSR's Soviet space program.

    1957 - 1969: The Space Race


    • Date: 1957 - 1969


    Events:

    • 1957: Sputnik 1 - First artificial satellite.

    • 1961: Yuri Gagarin - First human in space.

    • 1969: Apollo 11 - First manned moon landing.

    1970s - 1990s: Space Stations and Exploration


    • Date: 1970s - 1990s.


    Events:

    • 1971: Salyut 1 - First space station.

    • 1981: Space Shuttle - First reusable spacecraft.

    •1990s: International collaborations and space probes exploring the solar system.

    2000s - Present: International Space Cooperation


    • Date: 2000s - Present


    Events:

    • 2001: Chandrayaan-1 India's first lunar probe discovers water on the moon.

    • 2013: Mangalyaan ISRO successfully places its Mars orbiter in Mars orbit on its first attempt.

    • 2020s: Artemis program and return to the Moon plans.
